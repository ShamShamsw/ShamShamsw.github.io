BA SAP Notes

Business Analyst study notes for SAP and SAP Activate practices

Project description

A focused collection of Business Analyst study notes and reference material oriented to SAP and SAP Activate practices. The content spans BA fundamentals, SAP modules, delivery frameworks, SDLC considerations, and practical modeling examples like Order-to-Cash in SAP Signavio. The repository is designed as a consolidated learning and reference hub for analysts preparing for SAP projects.
