OpenSource as Infrastructure

Published: May 12, 2026

Author: Jacob T. Haseman

You may copy or reference this article for academic purposes with correct authorship attribution and citation.

Preview

I recently read an article on GitHub called "Software as a public good," and it got the wheels in my brain spinning. The author argues that open source is more than a way to build software. It is part of the foundation that keeps our digital world going.

What stood out to me is how open source supports almost every area, from businesses and governments to nonprofits and global organizations. When software is used everywhere, it becomes more than just code. It becomes a shared public resource.
