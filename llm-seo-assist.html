LLM-SEO-ASSIST

Private tooling that scales SEO workflows with LLMs

Professional description

LLM-SEO-ASSIST is a private tooling project that applies large language models to streamline and scale SEO workflows. The program helps teams generate SEO-focused content (titles, meta descriptions, H1 and H2 suggestions), produce content briefs, perform on-page optimization checks, suggest internal linking and schema markup, and create audit reports tied to measurable SEO signals. It is designed to integrate LLM outputs with SEO best practices and analytics data so businesses can produce consistent, search-optimized content at scale while preserving editorial control.
