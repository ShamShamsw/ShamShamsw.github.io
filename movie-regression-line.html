Movie Budget Analysis

Movie budgets and their lifetime gross with insightful visualizations.

Description

This project analyzes movie budgets using Matplotlib. It showcases data with a Histogram of Movie Budgets, explores frequency and budget distribution, and includes a Linear Regression line to highlight the relationship between Budget and Lifetime Gross.

Objectives Completed

  • Visualized movie budget distribution with histograms.
  • Analyzed frequency and budget trends.
  • Implemented a Linear Regression line for Budget vs. Lifetime Gross.
  • Provided clear and interactive data visualizations.
