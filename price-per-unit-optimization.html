Price Per Unit Optimization

Analyzing and optimizing pricing strategies for better profitability.

Description

This project focuses on analyzing pricing data to optimize price-per-unit strategies. It includes data visualization, statistical analysis, and actionable insights to improve decision-making.

Objectives Completed

  • Data cleaning and preprocessing for accurate analysis.
  • Visualization of pricing trends and patterns.
  • Statistical analysis of profitability metrics.
  • Development of predictive models for pricing optimization.
